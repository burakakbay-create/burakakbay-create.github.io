Frequently asked

  • How do I restore my “Remove Ads” purchase? Open Settings → Restore Purchases. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID.
  • Is my progress saved? Yes — your level progress and settings are saved on your device.
  • What languages are supported? 13: English, Turkish, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Hindi and Arabic.
  • Are there ads? Only non-personalized ads, and you can remove them with a one-time purchase.
